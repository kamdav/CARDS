How to join CARDS

We welcome new members at any time.  Members should be Chartered Accountants and in good standing with The Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales, the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Scotland or the Institute of Chartered Accountants in Ireland. We also accept members from any other chartered accountancy body which is part of Chartered Accountants Worldwide, such as the Institutes in Australia, New Zealand, Canada, India and South Africa.

Candidates should have a proposer and a seconder, both of whom are CARDS members – but if you do not have these and you otherwise qualify then do not be deterred!  Our secretary, David Lindsay, will arrange suitable introductions for you.

Please complete an application form, which you may download here.

Please send the completed form to David at dottylindsay@gmail.com or by post to him at

“The Far side”,
Bakers Row,

Maidenhead SL6 6PX

If you have a proposer and seconder, we will accept their signatures on separate copies of the form if that would be more convenient.

Do not hesitate to contact David if you have any questions about CARDS or about the joining procedure.

We look forward to welcoming you to CARDS.
