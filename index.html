Welcome to CARDS

CARDS is a Dining Club which meets four times per year.  The meetings take place in February, May, October and November.  It provides an opportunity for Chartered Accountants to spend a convivial evening with their colleagues and guests.

It was founded in 1979 as an alternate organisation to the then Chartered Accountants Dining Society which was run on very exclusive lines with membership confined to Members of Council and certain members in practice.  CARDS is open to Chartered Accountants in all fields and therefore enjoys a more diverse membership.

Current members include several influential figures in the profession and some past and current Council members, as well as members who are less active in the affairs of the Institute itself but are nevertheless interested in its progress and enjoy the company of other members on a social basis.

The venue for CARDS meetings is The Royal Thames Yacht Club, Knightsbridge, London SW1X 7LF.  Dress for gentlemen is black tie; and for ladies formal or business attire, as they prefer.
